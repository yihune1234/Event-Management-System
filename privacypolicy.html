EventHub

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 2, 2026

This policy explains how EventHub collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the website, sign up for an account, register for events, or manage events through the dashboards.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account details such as username, email address, phone number, and role.
  • Event details such as titles, descriptions, locations, organizers, and dates.
  • Registration data such as event selections and registration timestamps.
  • Support or contact details that you choose to send to us.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To process event registrations and cancellations.
  • To manage events, notifications, and dashboard features.
  • To maintain system security and investigate misuse.

3. Data Sharing

EventHub does not sell user information.

  • Information may be shared with administrators for system operation and event management.
  • Information may be disclosed when required by law or to protect the service.
  • Information may be stored in the app database to support registrations and notifications.

4. Data Security

We use server-side session checks and password hashing to help protect accounts. As with any web application, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain user and event information only as long as needed for account management, event administration, reporting, or legal obligations.

6. Your Choices

  • You can update your profile information from the user dashboard.
  • You can change your password through your account settings.
  • You can cancel event registrations from the user dashboard.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of EventHub after changes are posted means you accept the revised policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this policy or how your information is handled, please contact the project team.
